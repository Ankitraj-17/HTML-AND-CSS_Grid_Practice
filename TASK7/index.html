Answers

1. Why are template areas easy to understand?

Template areas use names like "header" and "footer" which are easier to read than numbers.

2. Which section spans full width?

Header and Footer span full width because they repeat twice in the template.

3. Can we rearrange layout by changing area names?

Yes, we can swap sidebar and content by changing their positions in grid-template-areas.

4. Is this better than column numbers?

Yes, for complex layouts it is easier to understand and maintain.

5. Where are template areas mostly used?

Used for page layouts with header, sidebar, main content, and footer sections.
