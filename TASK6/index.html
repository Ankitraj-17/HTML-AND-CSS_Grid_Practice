Answers

1. What are grid lines?

Grid lines are invisible lines that divide the grid. A 3-column grid has 4 lines (1, 2, 3, 4).

2. How many columns does the item span?

Box 1 spans 2 columns (from line 1 to line 3).

3. What happens to other items?

Other items move to the next available space automatically.

4. Can two items overlap?

Yes, items can overlap if you give them the same grid-column values.

5. Where is column spanning used in websites?

Used for headers, banners, featured content, and navigation bars.
